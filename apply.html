PhoenixAnarchy

The Donfuer PhoenixAnarchy branch is the best for those who seek an anarchy experience but also want access to new features. PhoenixAnarchy is the biggest 1.17 (soon to be 1.18) anarchy server and averages 30-40 players on at a time, even maintaining 20 tps despite having a non-strict anticheat.
Stoneworks

One of the newest editions to the DonFuer Family, the Stoneworks server offers DonFuerians a unique roleplay experience, reminiscent of DonFuer's earlier days. If anarchy isn't for you this is the place to go.
Minewind

Donfuer Minewind Branch is our newest branch, featuring multiple maps, player tp, and too many other special gameplay features to mention here. Mostly focused on megabuilds, Minewind is for those talented builders looking for a challenge.
